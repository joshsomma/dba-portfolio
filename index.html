Daniel Bou-Antoun

My name is Daniel Bou-Antoun. I’m a simple guy. I like to play games and I like to make games. While I was studying games design at University of Arts London, I learnt a great deal in how to create 3D/2D objects and animations in 3DS MAX. I also gained a good understanding of programming in Unity. After graduating last summer, with a first honours degree, I still spend a good portion of my time coding in Unity and seeing the different things that I can create. I like puzzle games that challenge the player’s mind and gives them a experience that they will remember. That's all I've ever wanted to do, create games for people to enjoy.
